Development tools for mobile first, cloud first Mobile apps are often supported by services in the cloud. Satya Nadella, CEO of Microsoft, famously said the following: To me, when we say mobile first, it’s not the mobility of the device, it’s actually the mobility of the individual experience. [...] The only way you are going to be able to orchestrate the mobility of these applications and data is through the cloud.
